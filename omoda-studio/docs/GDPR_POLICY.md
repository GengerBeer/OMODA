# GDPR Policy

**Controller:** Alatyr Systems LLC  
**Service:** OMODA STUDIO  
**Last updated:** April 6, 2026

## 1. General Provisions

This GDPR Policy describes the approach used by Alatyr Systems LLC when processing personal data in connection with the OMODA STUDIO service. The purpose of this document is to explain, in a structured and practical form, what categories of personal data may be processed, on what grounds such processing may occur, how long the data may be retained, what rights data subjects may exercise, and what organizational safeguards are applied in the course of processing.

This document is intended to supplement the public Privacy Policy published on the Site. In the event that a separate contract, data processing agreement, or mandatory legal requirement imposes stricter obligations, such stricter obligation shall prevail to the extent required by law.

## 2. Scope Of Application

This Policy applies to personal data processed by Alatyr Systems LLC in connection with:

- access to and use of the OMODA STUDIO website;
- submission of visual references, prompts, and other materials through the service interface;
- storage, retrieval, organization, and delivery of generated visual materials;
- technical operation of the service, including logging, error diagnosis, performance monitoring, and security review;
- handling of support, legal, and privacy-related requests.

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites, tools, or platforms merely linked from the Site, except to the extent that such third parties act as processors or infrastructure providers engaged for the operation of the service.

## 3. Categories Of Personal Data

Depending on the manner in which the service is used, Alatyr Systems LLC may process the following categories of personal data:

- outfit references, garment photographs, layered look references, background references, and similar uploaded visual content;
- portrait photographs, face photographs, full-body photographs, and other user-submitted images that may depict an identifiable person;
- prompts, descriptive instructions, text inputs, and generation settings entered through the interface;
- output files and related status information created in the course of the requested processing;
- technical metadata associated with requests, including timestamps, identifiers, storage references, error data, and service logs;
- correspondence connected with support, legal requests, privacy inquiries, or operational follow-up.

Where uploaded or generated materials contain information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, such information is treated as personal data for the purposes of this Policy.

## 4. Sources Of Personal Data

Personal data may be obtained from the following sources:

- directly from the individual using the Site or acting on behalf of the relevant individual or organization;
- from visual materials uploaded through the interface;
- from prompts, text instructions, and generation parameters supplied by the user;
- from technical systems required to host, secure, and deliver the service.

Alatyr Systems LLC does not rely on hidden collection methods for the core functionality of the service. The principal data inputs are those intentionally submitted in the course of using OMODA STUDIO.

## 5. Purposes Of Processing

Personal data may be processed for the following purposes:

- to provide the functionality requested by the user through the service;
- to receive, store, and manage uploaded materials necessary for the requested workflow;
- to create, store, and return resulting visual outputs;
- to maintain the stability, security, and integrity of the service;
- to detect misuse, respond to incidents, and troubleshoot errors;
- to maintain internal records of requests, status changes, and system actions;
- to respond to lawful requests, legal claims, or regulatory obligations;
- to handle support and privacy-related communications.

Personal data is not processed for a purpose materially incompatible with the purpose for which it was collected, unless a separate legal basis exists or the applicable law otherwise permits such further processing.

## 6. Legal Bases For Processing

Where Regulation (EU) 2016/679 applies, Alatyr Systems LLC may rely on one or more of the following legal bases, depending on the circumstances:

### 6.1 Contractual Necessity

Processing may be necessary in order to take steps at the request of the data subject prior to entering into a contract or to perform obligations connected with the requested service. This includes receiving reference materials, processing instructions, generating requested outputs, and making such outputs available.

### 6.2 Legitimate Interests

Processing may be necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by Alatyr Systems LLC, provided that such interests are not overridden by the interests or fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject. Such legitimate interests may include:

- service security;
- abuse prevention;
- operational logging;
- diagnostics and troubleshooting;
- infrastructure monitoring;
- recordkeeping related to platform reliability and continuity.

### 6.3 Legal Obligation

Processing may also be necessary to comply with legal obligations applicable to Alatyr Systems LLC, including obligations relating to lawful disclosure, record retention, regulatory cooperation, or dispute handling.

### 6.4 Consent

Where the nature of the submitted material or the applicable law makes consent the appropriate basis, processing may rely on consent. This is particularly relevant where an individual voluntarily uploads photographs depicting themselves for a personalization workflow. Consent may be withdrawn to the extent permitted by law, but withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before such withdrawal.

## 7. Special Category Data

Some visual materials uploaded through the service may, depending on their content and context, reveal sensitive information or fall within special categories of personal data under Article 9 GDPR. This may occur, for example, where an uploaded image clearly reveals biometric, health-related, ethnic, religious, or similarly sensitive information.

Alatyr Systems LLC does not request special category data as a standalone business purpose. However, such data may be incidentally included in user-submitted visual materials. In such cases:

- processing is limited to what is necessary for the requested service, security, and compliance purposes;
- access is restricted to the extent reasonably possible within the technical operation of the platform;
- the data is not used for a separate unrelated profiling purpose;
- the data is handled under the same confidentiality and security expectations that apply to other personal data, with heightened caution due to its nature.

Where a specific legal basis is required for the processing of special category data, Alatyr Systems LLC relies on the basis that is most appropriate under the circumstances and applicable law.

## 8. Automated Processing

OMODA STUDIO includes automated processing workflows that operate on uploaded references and user instructions in order to produce requested outputs. Such automated processing is part of the technical functionality of the service.

However, the service is not designed to make solely automated decisions that produce legal effects concerning a data subject or similarly significantly affect a data subject within the meaning of Article 22 GDPR.

## 9. Processors And Service Providers

For the technical operation of the service, Alatyr Systems LLC may use infrastructure and software providers acting as processors or sub-processors. At the date of this Policy, the principal providers supporting the service include:

- **Supabase**, for database hosting, storage, authentication-related infrastructure, and operational records;
- **Vercel**, for web hosting, serverless execution, and delivery infrastructure;
- **Google Gemini**, for image-processing and content-generation functionality requested by users.

Such providers are engaged only to the extent required to operate the service. Access to personal data by those providers is limited by the architecture of the service, their own service role, and applicable contractual or technical safeguards.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Because infrastructure and software providers may operate internationally, personal data may be processed in jurisdictions outside the country from which the service is accessed and, where applicable, outside the EEA.

Where GDPR requires a lawful transfer mechanism, Alatyr Systems LLC relies on the transfer mechanism made available under the applicable provider relationship or otherwise required by law, including contractual safeguards where appropriate.

## 11. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including:

- delivery of the requested functionality;
- operational continuity;
- service security;
- troubleshooting and incident review;
- legal defense and regulatory compliance.

Retention periods may vary depending on the category of data, the system in which the data resides, applicable backup cycles, legal obligations, and whether a valid deletion request has been received and can be actioned without conflicting with another lawful basis for retention.

Where deletion is requested and the request is valid, deletion may be carried out from active systems first, while residual data may continue to exist temporarily in backups, logs, or disaster recovery archives until normal overwrite or expiration cycles apply.

## 12. Data Subject Rights

Subject to GDPR, its limitations, and any relevant exemptions, a data subject may have the following rights:

- the right to obtain confirmation as to whether personal data is being processed;
- the right to access personal data;
- the right to rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data;
- the right to erasure;
- the right to restriction of processing;
- the right to data portability where applicable;
- the right to object where processing is based on legitimate interests;
- the right to withdraw consent where consent is the basis relied upon.

Each request is assessed individually, taking into account the identity of the requester, the scope of the request, technical feasibility, the rights of others, legal retention obligations, and the extent to which the requested action is required by law.

## 13. Handling Of Requests

Requests concerning personal data should be submitted through the official company contact channels made available by Alatyr Systems LLC.

Before acting on a request, Alatyr Systems LLC may require information reasonably necessary to:

- verify the identity of the requester;
- confirm the scope of the requested action;
- distinguish the relevant records from other records stored in the system;
- ensure that any disclosure or deletion does not unlawfully affect the rights of another person.

Where a request is manifestly unfounded, excessive, or otherwise limited by law, Alatyr Systems LLC reserves the right to refuse the request or to respond as permitted by the applicable legal framework.

## 14. Security Measures

Alatyr Systems LLC applies reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure, or unauthorized access.

Such measures may include:

- access control and credential management;
- hosted infrastructure safeguards;
- controlled storage access;
- system monitoring and logging;
- routine troubleshooting and incident response procedures;
- role-based operational limitations where feasible.

No online platform can guarantee absolute security. Accordingly, while reasonable safeguards are used, absolute protection against all threats cannot be promised.

## 15. Complaints And Supervisory Authorities

Where GDPR applies, a data subject has the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority, in particular in the Member State of habitual residence, place of work, or place of the alleged infringement, if the data subject considers that the processing of personal data infringes applicable law.

Alatyr Systems LLC nevertheless encourages individuals to first use the available company channels so that a practical resolution may be attempted without unnecessary delay.

## 16. Amendments

This GDPR Policy may be updated from time to time in order to reflect changes in the service, legal requirements, infrastructure, or internal procedures. The then-current version shall be the governing version from the date of publication unless a later effective date is expressly stated.
