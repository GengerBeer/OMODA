# GDPR Policy And Data Processing Overview

**Controller:** Alatyr Systems LLC  
**Service:** OMODA STUDIO  
**Document version date:** April 6, 2026

## 1. Purpose Of This Document

This document describes, at a policy level, how personal data may be processed in connection with OMODA STUDIO. It is intended to summarize the categories of data involved, the purposes of processing, the legal bases that may apply, the general storage model, the categories of service providers engaged, and the rights available to data subjects under applicable law.

This document is intentionally written at a compliance and policy level. It does not disclose internal system structure, implementation details, database schemas, storage architecture, routing logic, or other operational specifics beyond what is reasonably necessary to explain the nature of processing.

## 2. Scope

This Policy applies to personal data processed by Alatyr Systems LLC in connection with:

- access to and use of the OMODA STUDIO website;
- submission of visual references, images, prompts, and related materials through the service;
- generation, storage, and delivery of resulting outputs;
- operation, security, and maintenance of the service;
- support, legal, and privacy-related communications.

## 3. Categories Of Personal Data

Depending on how the service is used, Alatyr Systems LLC may process the following categories of personal data:

- outfit and garment reference images;
- images depicting identifiable persons, including portrait, face, and full-body photographs where submitted;
- optional background images and related visual references;
- prompts, descriptive instructions, and generation preferences;
- generated visual outputs that may depict an identifiable person;
- account or profile information where account-based features are in use;
- technical and operational metadata necessary for service delivery, security, diagnostics, and continuity;
- correspondence relating to support, legal, or privacy matters.

Where uploaded or generated materials contain information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, that information is treated as personal data.

## 4. Sources Of Personal Data

Personal data may be obtained:

- directly from users of the service;
- from materials intentionally uploaded through the service interface;
- from prompts, settings, and instructions entered by users;
- from operational, hosting, storage, and security systems involved in delivering the service.

## 5. Purposes Of Processing

Personal data may be processed for the following purposes:

- to provide the requested virtual try-on or image-generation functionality;
- to receive, store, organize, and process submitted materials;
- to create and return generated outputs;
- to maintain service integrity, availability, and security;
- to prevent abuse, investigate incidents, and troubleshoot failures;
- to satisfy legal, regulatory, or dispute-related obligations;
- to handle support and privacy-related requests.

## 6. Legal Bases

Where Regulation (EU) 2016/679 applies, processing may rely on one or more of the following legal bases, depending on the circumstances:

- contractual necessity or steps taken at the request of the data subject;
- legitimate interests, including service security, diagnostics, abuse prevention, and continuity of operations;
- legal obligation where disclosure, retention, or cooperation is required by law;
- consent where the nature of the submitted content or the applicable legal framework makes consent the appropriate basis.

## 7. Sensitive Or Special Category Data

Some submitted images may reveal information that is sensitive in nature or may fall within special categories of personal data depending on their content and context. Alatyr Systems LLC does not request such information as a separate business purpose, but it may be incidentally included in visual materials uploaded by users.

Where such content is processed, the intention is to limit processing to what is reasonably necessary for service delivery, security, and compliance purposes.

## 8. General Storage Model

Personal data processed through OMODA STUDIO may be stored within:

- application databases;
- object or file storage used for submitted and generated images;
- operational hosting and delivery infrastructure;
- logging and monitoring systems reasonably required for security, diagnostics, and continuity.

This document does not enumerate internal storage structures or technical identifiers.

## 9. Categories Of Recipients And Service Providers

In order to operate OMODA STUDIO, Alatyr Systems LLC may use third-party providers acting as infrastructure providers, processors, or sub-processors. These may include providers responsible for:

- cloud database and file storage services;
- website hosting and delivery infrastructure;
- AI-based image or media generation services;
- internal or proprietary generation pipelines used by or on behalf of Alatyr Systems LLC.

At the time of writing, the service may involve providers or processing categories that include:

- Supabase;
- Vercel;
- Google Gemini;
- ByteDance;
- Kling;
- proprietary internal pipelines.

The fact that a provider category is listed here does not necessarily mean that every such provider is active in every request or every deployment at all times. The purpose of this section is to avoid under-disclosure where the processing model may involve multiple generation or infrastructure providers over time.

## 10. Data Shared With Service Providers

Depending on the selected workflow and the service configuration, personal data shared with infrastructure or generation providers may include:

- submitted reference images;
- portrait or body images where provided by the user;
- prompts and generation instructions;
- generated outputs;
- technical request metadata necessary to operate, secure, or troubleshoot the service.

Data is intended to be shared only to the extent reasonably necessary for hosting, storage, generation, delivery, monitoring, or related support functions.

## 11. International Transfers

Because cloud, hosting, delivery, and AI-generation providers may operate internationally, personal data may be processed outside the country from which the service is accessed and, where applicable, outside the EEA.

Where GDPR requires a lawful transfer mechanism, Alatyr Systems LLC intends to rely on the relevant lawful basis or transfer mechanism made available under the applicable provider relationship and legal framework.

## 12. Retention

Personal data may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to:

- provide the requested functionality;
- maintain service continuity and security;
- investigate incidents or technical issues;
- respond to lawful requests or disputes;
- comply with applicable legal obligations.

Retention periods may vary depending on the category of data, the nature of the workflow, operational needs, provider limitations, backup cycles, and whether a valid deletion request has been received.

Residual copies of data may continue to exist for a limited period in logs, backups, monitoring systems, or disaster recovery systems even after deletion from active environments.

## 13. Security

Alatyr Systems LLC applies reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, loss, or destruction of personal data.

Such measures may include access limitation, credential management, provider-side infrastructure safeguards, operational review, logging, monitoring, and incident response procedures appropriate to the nature of the service.

No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

## 14. Data Subject Rights

Subject to applicable law, a data subject may have the right to request:

- confirmation of whether personal data is processed;
- access to personal data;
- correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
- deletion;
- restriction of processing;
- portability where applicable;
- objection where processing is based on legitimate interests;
- withdrawal of consent where consent is the relevant legal basis.

Requests are assessed in light of identity verification, scope, legal limitations, technical feasibility, the rights of others, and any retention obligations that may continue to apply.

## 15. Handling Of Requests

Requests concerning personal data should be submitted through the official company channels made available by Alatyr Systems LLC.

Before acting on a request, the company may require information reasonably necessary to verify identity, clarify scope, and distinguish the relevant data from other records.

## 16. Complaints

Where GDPR applies, a data subject may lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority if the data subject considers that the processing of personal data infringes applicable law.

## 17. Updates

This document may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements, provider relationships, processing models, or service operations. The then-current published version shall govern from the date of publication unless a later effective date is expressly stated.
