## 1. Purpose

This GDPR Policy describes how Alatyr Systems LLC processes personal data in connection with OMODA STUDIO, including uploaded images, prompts, generated assets, technical records, and related support or legal requests.

## 2. Data Categories

OMODA STUDIO may process:

- uploaded garment, outfit, face, body, and background images;
- prompts, model descriptions, and generation settings;
- generated outputs and related status metadata;
- technical logs, timestamps, identifiers, and storage references;
- communications relating to support, privacy, or compliance.

## 3. Purposes Of Processing

We process personal data to:

- provide AI-assisted virtual try-on and return generated outputs;
- host, store, retrieve, and organize user-submitted assets;
- maintain security, prevent abuse, diagnose failures, and improve reliability;
- respond to legal, privacy, and support inquiries;
- comply with applicable law.

## 4. Legal Bases Under GDPR

Where GDPR applies, the principal legal bases used are:

- contract / pre-contractual necessity;
- legitimate interests for security, operations, and troubleshooting;
- legal obligation where recordkeeping or disclosure is required;
- consent where users voluntarily submit personal imagery for requested personalization.

## 5. Special Category Data

Some uploaded photos may reveal information treated as special category or otherwise sensitive personal data depending on context. Where such content is voluntarily uploaded for generation, it is processed only to provide the requested service, secure the platform, and comply with law.

## 6. Automated Processing

OMODA STUDIO uses automated and AI-assisted systems to generate requested outputs. The service is not intended to make solely automated decisions producing legal or similarly significant effects about individuals within the meaning of GDPR Article 22.

## 7. Processors And Infrastructure

At the time of this Policy, the main service providers supporting OMODA STUDIO include:

- Supabase: database hosting, file storage, and operational records;
- Vercel: website hosting, function execution, and delivery;
- Google Gemini: AI-based image generation.

## 8. International Transfers

Because infrastructure and AI providers may operate internationally, personal data may be transferred outside the EEA. Where required, Alatyr Systems LLC relies on available lawful transfer mechanisms and contractual safeguards.

## 9. Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain security, investigate issues, support lawful requests, and satisfy compliance obligations. Retention may vary by system and backup cycle.

## 10. Data Subject Rights

Subject to GDPR and applicable limitations, individuals may request:

- access;
- rectification;
- erasure;
- restriction;
- portability where applicable;
- objection to processing based on legitimate interests;
- withdrawal of consent where consent is the applicable basis.

## 11. Exercising Rights

Requests should be sent to the published privacy contact of Alatyr Systems LLC and should include enough detail to identify the relevant data and verify the requester where necessary.

## 12. Complaints

Individuals may lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority in their country of habitual residence, place of work, or place of the alleged infringement.

## 13. Security

Alatyr Systems LLC applies reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to reduce unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or loss. No system can guarantee absolute security.
